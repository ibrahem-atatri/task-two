Ali Baba Restaurant

Ali Baba Restaurant in the city of Jenin is one of the most famous restaurants in the city, offering a blend of popular and Western cuisine. It is known for its central location on Military Street, near the Yahya Ayash Roundabout. This central location makes Ali Baba Restaurant a popular destination for locals and visitors looking to enjoy a diverse range of dishes while exploring the city of Jenin.
